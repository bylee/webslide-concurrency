Producer-Consumer

  • Safety - Less risk to break
    • Safe although server or client is slow
  • Liveness - Risk to wait permanently
    • No method to escape wait() without put method
    • Exclusion control between consumers
  • Performance - Bottle neck
    • Better modularity as aspect to the Queuing network model
  • Reusability - Increase reusability
    • Each RequestQueue instances have the responsibility for thread control.
